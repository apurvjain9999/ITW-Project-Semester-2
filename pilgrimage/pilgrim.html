Pilgrimage Sites

The state of Uttarakhand is popularly known as Devbhoomi (Land of the Gods) due to the presence of numerous pilgrimage sites. As a result, religious tourism forms a major portion of the tourism in the state.



  • Char Dham
  • Piran Kaliyar
  • Hemkund Sahib
  • Haridwar
  • Rishikesh
  • Neelkanth Mahadev
