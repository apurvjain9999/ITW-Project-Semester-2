Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy of Administration


The Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy of Administration (LBSNAA) is a research and training institute on public policy and public administration in India. Operated by India's Union Government, LBSNAA is located in the remote town of Mussoorie, Uttarakhand in the foothills of the Himalayas.

The academy's main purpose is to train civil service officers of Indian Administrative Service, Indian Police Service, Indian Forest Service; and also for Group-A Central Services such as Indian Foreign Service among others.

The Indian Administrative Service (IAS) trainees officers are awarded an MA degree in Public Management from Jawaharlal Nehru University, Delhi.
