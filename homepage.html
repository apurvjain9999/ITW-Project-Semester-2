About Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is the Best Place for Nature Lovers. It is the 27th state of India situated in North India. Dehradun is the Capital of Uttarakhand. States like Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana, Himanchal Pradesh, and countries like Nepal, Tibet are in the neighbourhood of Uttarakhand. It was extracted from Uttar Pradesh on 9th November 2000. Many a times you hear about Devbhoomi , it is the Second Name of Uttarakhand, which means "Land of the God". It is origin of two holy rivers namely: Ganga from Gangotri and Yamuna from Yamunotri. This dream destination consists of Natural Beauty with Pure Water, Large Mountains, Snow falls, Lakes etc. If you want to see Heaven on Earth make a tour of Uttarakhand. Uttarakhand has three Airports namely Jolly Grant Airport at Dehradun and Pantnagar Airport at Udham Singh Nagar District for Civil Aviation and Chakrata Base Airport at Dehradun which is basically used by Air force.

The Population of Uttarakhand is 10,116,752 according to the Census of 2011. It clearly shows that Uttarakhand is the 19th Most Populous State of India. A big part of population belong to Villages and some of them live very difficult life. Only 12% of this State's is land area, another 88% is Hilly area. A big part of the population, approximately 70% depends on agriculture. Hindu, Muslim, Sikh, Christians, Buddhists and Jains all are living here but 88% of total population are Hindus and Muslims. BJP (Bharatiya Janta Party) and Congress are the two main political parties in Uttarakhand.

Kumaoni and Garhwali, the dialects of Hindi or more broadly Hindustani, are the regional languages of Uttarakhand. Haldwani, Kotdwar, Srinagar (Garhwal), Ramnagar are the cites which serve as the Gateway to the hilly regions of Uttarakhand. Kotdwar and Srinagar are on the entrance of the Garhwal hills and Nainital and Ramnagar on the Kumaon hills. High court of Uttarakhand is situated at Nainital. Uttarakhand is divided in two parts one is Kumaon, and another is Garhwal.

There are many Institutions in Uttrakhand, namely: Indian Military Academy (IMA) and Rashtriya Military College at Dehradun; IIT Roorkee, CBRI and NIH at Roorkee; and Lal Bahadur Shastri College of IAS Training at Mussorie.
