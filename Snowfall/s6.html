Ranikhet – Sprawling in the Queen’s land


A cantonment town in the Almora district of Uttarakhand, Ranikhet, also known as the "Switzerland of India", is a popular getaway for couples and families. The scenic walking trails, coupled with local sightseeing and adventure sports, make Ranikhet one of the prime destinations in Uttarakhand.

Things to do:Paragliding, local sightseeing, and taking long walks
