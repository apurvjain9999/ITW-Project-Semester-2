Auli – The winds of winter


A favorite skiing destination, Auli is among the best places to visit in Uttarakhand in December. The ski slopes of Auli rise from 2,500 to 3,050 meters above sea level and both amateur and professional ski enthusiasts head to Auli for a rejuvenating holiday experience. The awe-inspiring & panoramic views of some of the highest Himalayan peaks – Nanda Devi, Kamet, Mana Parvat, and Dunagiri – make Auli a much sought after destination in India.

Things to do:Skiing, trekking, gondola rides, and camping.
