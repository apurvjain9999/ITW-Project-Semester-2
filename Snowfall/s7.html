Situated in the Pithoragarh district, Munsiyari is the base of the Himalayan mountain range. It is popular among high altitude trekkers and adventure enthusiasts. The Diverse flora and fauna, the misty weather, and the enticing Himalayas should be enough to give Munsiyari a place on your bucket list. Among the enchanting winter destinations in Uttarakhand, Munsiyari is highly recommended.

Things to do:Camping, trekking, skiing, snowboarding, and photography
