Changing with seasons, terrain, and time of the day, Jim Corbett National Park is among the foremost places to visit in Uttarakhand in winter. The reserve is split into various zones and tourists can decide which zone to visit based on their interests. Where Dhikala is the largest and is known for its vast grasslands, Durgadevi and Sonanadi engage tourists with jeep safaris. The Jhirna zone is preferred by birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts.

Things to do: Jeep safaris, elephant rides, unplanned excursions, wildlife photography, and bonfire nights
